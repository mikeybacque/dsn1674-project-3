CREPES

Our French crepes are thin and delicate pancakes that can be filled with almost anything you like, from fresh fruit, chocolate sauce, and whipped cream — to brie, mushrooms and spinach. Perfect for breakfast, lunch, or dinner.

CHOCOLATE

Made of cocoa paste and pure cocoa butter, our gourmet dark chocolate is a real delight for the senses! Our chocolatiers use only the freshest ingredients for their sophisticated chocolate creations.

COFFEE

Our passion for coffee is rivaled only by our love of sharing it. We source our coffee from Happy Goat Coffee Company, whose mission is to offer the highest quality artisan, fairly traded organic coffee from around the world. We guarantee the freshest coffee every time, and we don’t like to brag, but we’ve been told our Americano is the best in the city!

EVENTS

Connect with the community at one of our weekly events! We host music performances, poetry readings, and other exciting events that help you stay in touch with the diverse culture of Ottawa.
